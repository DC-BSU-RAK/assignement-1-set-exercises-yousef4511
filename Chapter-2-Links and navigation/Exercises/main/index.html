Plot

Stranger Things is a supernatural sci-fi thriller set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. A young boy named Will goes missing under mysterious circumstances, leading his friends and family to uncover a secret government lab, a girl with psychokinetic powers, and a parallel dimension known as the Upside Down.

Characters

Mike Wheeler: A brave and loyal friend, who becomes close to Eleven.

Will Byers: The boy who disappears into the Upside Down.

Jim Hopper: The town’s police chief who uncovers dark secrets beneath Hawkins.

Joyce Byers: Will’s determined and loving mother.
