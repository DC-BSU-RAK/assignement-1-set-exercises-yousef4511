Pizza

pizza is an delicious italian dish consisting of a round shape dough made with tomatoes and cheese with toppings like meat and vegatables.

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces of pizza dough
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic,minced
  • 1/2 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1/2dried rosemary,crushed
  • salt to taste
  • Mozzarella cheese

Method

  1. Activate the yeast by mixing warm water and sugar. Sprinkle the yeast and wait for it to foam (5-10 minutes).
  2. Combine flour, salt, olive oil, and the activated yeast mixture. Knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise for about 1-1.5 hours, until it doubles in size.
  4. Prepare the sauce by cooking garlic in olive oil, then add crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven.
  6. Roll out the dough into a circle, spread sauce, sprinkle cheese, and add your toppings.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Serve hot, garnish with fresh basil, and enjoy!
